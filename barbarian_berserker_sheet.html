RAGE USES
RAGE
Bonus Action or Reaction (to taking damage). Lasts 1 min. Advantage on STR checks/saves. +PB melee STR damage. Resistance to bludgeoning, piercing, slashing.
Powerful Throw: Add Rage Damage to thrown weapon attacks using STR.
Lv7: Advantage on CON saves during rage.
Reckless Attack
First attack of your turn: choose to attack recklessly. Advantage on melee STR attacks this turn, but attacks against you have advantage until your next turn.
FOCUSED RECKLESS
Advantage on first attack only. Enemies do NOT gain advantage against you. Cannot use Brutal Strike on this turn. PB uses per Long Rest.
Resilient Rage
RESILIENT RAGE
Reaction when taking damage while raging: gain Resistance to that damage type until rage ends. CON mod uses per Long Rest.

Brutal Strike
When you use Reckless Attack (not Focused), you can forgo advantage on one attack to deal extra damage.
Damage: +1d10 + STR
Crit Range: 19-20
Brutal Mastery (Lv13): Choose 2 riders instead of 1 (1/short rest). +PB to Brutal Strike damage.
Rider Effects (choose 1)
Forceful Blow. Push target 15 ft. If it hits something, 1d6 bludgeon to both.
Hamstring Blow. Target speed reduced by 15 ft until start of your next turn.
Staggering Blow. Target has disadvantage on next saving throw.
Sundering Blow. Next attack roll vs target gains +PB.
Hemorrhage. Target takes Rage Damage as bleed at start of its next turn (nonstacking, ends with magical healing).
Expose (Lv13). Target's AC reduced by 2 against you and chosen creatures until start of your next turn.
Brutal Strike unlocks at Level 9.

Berserker Features
FRENZY (Lv3)
If you use Reckless Attack while raging, deal extra damage to the first target hit on your turn with a STR attack. Roll PB d6s — same damage type as the weapon.
MINDLESS RAGE (Lv6)
Immunity to Charmed and Frightened while raging. If Charmed or Frightened when entering rage, the condition ends.
RETALIATION (Lv10)
When you take damage from a creature within 5 ft, you can take a Reaction to make one melee attack against that creature, using a weapon or Unarmed Strike.
INTIMIDATING PRESENCE (Lv14)
Bonus Action. Each creature of your choice in 30-ft Emanation: WIS save (DC 8 + STR mod + PB). Fail: Frightened for 1 minute (repeats save each turn). 1/Long Rest, or expend a Rage use to restore it.
